About Us

Vincode Technology

Vincode Technology, founded in the year 2016,in Patan provides Integrated Cloud Communications Solutions across SMS, Voice and Email. The strong and intensifying belief in the power of cloud communications, along with the need for Innovation to provide these services in a uniquely simple and integrated way, is what the company thrives on every day. Vincode Technology provides a complete range of communication services through APIs that customers can directly integrate into their applications,and customized solutions tailor made to business-specific needs.

Vincode Technology is a one-stop solution for all your digital communication needs. It is a cost effective end-to-end bulk SMS & email marketing provider with high service level standards that is unmatched in the industry. Vincode Technology provides both 1-Way & 2-Way SMS communication including SMS Push, short code and long code services, SMS gateway with APIs, SMS Excel plugin, SMS contest, voting & polling and Voice SMS services.

Among our clients include Leading real estate companies, Auto manufacturers & dealers, Agro-product distributors, Banks & insurance companies, Consumer durables, Hospitals, Hotels, Schools, Stock brokers, Telecom operators, Utilities and Web developers have been using our services year on year basis.
